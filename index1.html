01 About My Journey

My name is Camila Fonseca. I am a psychologist who believes in the wisdom of lived experience.

Before settling in Southern India, I traveled through Latin America for 3.5 years, journeying by land and water. I lived in meditation centers, learned from indigenous communities, and climbed mountains to find silence.

Now, I combine my clinical training (IFS, Somatic Experiencing) with the deep empathy learned on the road to help you navigate your own internal terrain.
