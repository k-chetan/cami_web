01 About Me

I am a psychologist graduated from the University of Brasília (UnB). Beyond books, I realized I needed to transcend my comfort zone to truly understand the human experience.

I traveled through Latin America for more than 3.5 years by land and water (from Argentina to Guatemala), living in extreme situations and connecting with diverse realities. I also lived exclusively in meditation centers in Brazil and India for 2 years, deepening my knowledge through inner observation.

Upon becoming a mother, I faced my own complex trauma, which led me to study IFS and Somatic Experiencing deeply. Currently, I live in Southern India, where I see clients online while enjoying life among sadhus, monkeys, and the Arunachala mountain.

Qualifications
