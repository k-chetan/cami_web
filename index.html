About My Journey

I am a psychologist who believes in the wisdom of lived experience. Upon becoming a psychologist (UnB), I realized I needed to transcend the boundaries of my comfort zone.

I traveled through Latin America for more than 3.5 years by land and water, from Argentina to Guatemala. I lived in extreme situations and learned from diverse realities.

I also dedicated 2 years to living exclusively in meditation centers in Brazil and India. Now, being a mother, I have deeply studied trauma approaches (IFS, SE) to heal my own complex trauma and help others do the same.
