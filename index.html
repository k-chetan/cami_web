Psychotherapy For You Wherever You Are

Welcome to my virtual office. My name is Camila Fonseca.

I know that in the rush of everyday life, self-care is not always a priority. If you're feeling tired, confused, depressed or anxious, know that therapy is like taking a path back home.

We connect with our internal GPS while we travel the territory of who we are. We tune into our emotional needs and learn to take transformative actions.

About My Journey

I am a psychologist who believes in the wisdom of the road. After graduating from UnB, I traveled through Latin America for 3.5 years, learning from indigenous communities and silence.

Now living in Southern India, I combine my clinical training (IFS, Somatic Experiencing) with deep empathy to help you navigate your own internal terrain.
