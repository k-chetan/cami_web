Who is Therapy For?

Therapy generally helps to increase self-knowledge, to manage interpersonal difficulties such as romantic, family or professional relationships.

Psychotherapy is a safe space to dive into the origin of certain patterns of emotions and also to process challenges in the present moment.

My

Methods

I use internationally recognized therapy models such as DBR and IFS. Both are non-pathologizing approaches incorporating memory reconsolidation.

I continuously update myself through readings from key figures like Bessel van der Kolk & Gabor Maté.

Deep Brain Reorienting (DBR)

Focuses on the brainstem's orienting response to trauma.

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

IFS is a compassionate and empowering approach that helps you understand and heal different parts.

Somatic Experiencing (SE)

Trauma is stored in the body's physiological responses. SE helps the nervous system regain balance.

Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)

Emotions are not symptoms, but messengers that some emotional need is not being met.
