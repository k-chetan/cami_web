Who is Therapy For?

Therapy helps to increase self-knowledge, manage interpersonal difficulties, and relieve tension.

It is a safe space to dive into the origin of patterns and process challenges in the present moment.

My Methods

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

We all have internal "parts". Some protect us, others hold pain. IFS is a compassionate approach that helps you heal these parts by bringing curiosity and self-compassion to your inner world.

Compassion • Healing • Harmony

Somatic Experiencing (SE)

Focuses on the body's wisdom. By observing bodily sensations, we help the nervous system gradually regain its natural balance after trauma or stress.

Body • Nervous System • Balance

Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT)

A humanistic approach where therapy takes place in an "ocean" of empathy. We use the power of emotion to transform pain into resilience.

Emotion • Empathy • Change
